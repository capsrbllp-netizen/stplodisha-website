Careers at Siba Toughened

Why Work With Us

Grow With a Company Scaling Up

Siba Toughened Private Limited is in active growth mode — transitioning from a trusted proprietorship legacy into an organised, professionally governed manufacturing company. That means real opportunities to grow into bigger roles as we expand our manufacturing capacity and distribution network across Odisha.

  • Hands-on experience across manufacturing, fabrication, sales, and logistics
  • Work directly with an experienced founder-director leadership team
  • Be part of a company built on two decades of regional industry trust
