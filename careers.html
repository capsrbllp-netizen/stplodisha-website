Why Join Us

Build Your Career with Siba Toughened Private Limited

At Siba Toughened Private Limited, we believe that our people are the foundation of our success. As one of Odisha's emerging manufacturers of premium toughened glass solutions, we are creating opportunities for talented individuals to learn, grow, and build rewarding careers.

With over two decades of industry experience and a strong vision for expansion, we offer a dynamic workplace where innovation, teamwork, and professional excellence are valued every day.
