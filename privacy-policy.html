Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Siba Toughened Private Limited ("Company," "we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through www.stplodisha.com (the "Website"), how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our Contact form, we collect the information you provide: name, phone number, email address, the product/service you are interested in, and any message or project details you choose to share. We do not collect payment information through this Website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted is used solely to respond to your enquiry, prepare quotations, and provide product or order-related support. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Enquiry details are transmitted securely and stored only for as long as necessary to address your enquiry and maintain reasonable business records, in line with applicable Indian data protection requirements.

This Website may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand overall visitor traffic (e.g. pages viewed). No personally identifiable browsing data is sold or shared with advertisers.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have submitted to us by emailing sibatoughened@gmail.com.
