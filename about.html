Our Story

From a Trusted Glass House to an Organised Manufacturing Enterprise

Siba Toughened Private Limited is an Odisha-incorporated company engaged in the manufacturing of toughened safety glass and wholesale distribution of glass products. The company is promoted by the founder of Siba Glass House, a trusted name in the glass trade for over two decades across Odisha.

This private limited company is established to scale the legacy of Siba Glass House into an organised, infrastructure-backed manufacturing and distribution enterprise — combining hands-on industry expertise with professional management across finance, operations, liaison, and governance.

  • Manufacturing unit with modern toughening furnace infrastructure
  • Wholesale distribution network spanning dealers & contractors across Odisha
  • Board-governed entity with dedicated finance, operations & liaison leadership
