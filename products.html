Safety Glass

Toughened Glass

Heat-treated safety glass processed by controlled thermal treatment to enhance strength — 4 to 5 times stronger than standard annealed glass. When broken, it fragments into small, granular pieces rather than sharp shards, dramatically reducing injury risk.

  • 4–5× the strength of annealed glass
  • Fractures into granular, low-risk pieces
  • High resistance to thermal & impact stress

Multi-Layer

Laminated Glass

Two or more glass sheets bonded with a tough polymer interlayer (PVB). Even if it breaks, fragments stay bonded to the interlayer, keeping the pane largely intact — offering superior security, sound insulation, and UV protection.

  • Stays intact on impact — strong security barrier
  • Reduces external noise transmission
  • Blocks up to 99% of UV radiation
BalustradesCanopiesSkylightsHigh-Security Glazing
Energy Efficient

Insulated / Double-Glazed Glass

A prefabricated unit of two or more glass panes separated by a sealed air or gas-filled gap. This design significantly reduces heat transfer, cuts cooling/heating costs, and improves acoustic insulation for buildings.

  • Lower energy bills through thermal insulation
  • Reduces condensation and outside noise
  • Ideal for facades & climate-controlled interiors
