About the Company

From a Trusted Glass House to an Organised Manufacturing Enterprise

Siba Toughened Private Limited is an Odisha-incorporated company engaged in the manufacturing of toughened safety glass and wholesale distribution of glass products. The company is promoted by the founder of Siba Glass House, a trusted name in the glass trade for over two decades across Odisha.

This private limited company scales that legacy into an organised, infrastructure-backed manufacturing and distribution enterprise — combining hands-on industry expertise with professional management across finance, operations, liaison, and governance.
